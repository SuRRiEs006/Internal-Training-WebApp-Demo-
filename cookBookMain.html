FOOD

Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Directions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. In another bowl, beat the milk, egg, and melted butter together.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until combined.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  6. Brown on both sides and serve hot.

Omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced tomatoes, spinach, mushrooms

Directions

  1. Beat the eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and cook for a few minutes until the edges start to lift from the pan.
  4. Add fillings to one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold the other half over the fillings and cook until the cheese melts and the eggs are set.

French Toast

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Butter for frying

Directions

  1. Whisk together eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon.
  2. Dip each slice of bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  3. Heat butter in a skillet and cook the bread slices on medium heat until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Serve hot with syrup or powdered sugar.

Grilled Chicken Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Lettuce, tomato, and mayonnaise

Directions

  1. Season the chicken breast with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill the chicken until fully cooked and juicy.
  3. Assemble the sandwich with bread, lettuce, tomato slices, mayonnaise, and the grilled chicken.
  4. Serve immediately.

Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 200g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • Salt, pepper, and herbs to taste

Directions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook ground beef with onion and garlic until browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce and season with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  4. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti.

Cinnamon Roll

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 4 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons yeast
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/3 cup butter, softened

Directions

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm milk in a large bowl.
  2. Add sugar, butter, salt, eggs, and flour; mix well.
  3. Knead the dough into a large ball, using your hands dusted lightly with flour.
  4. Cover and let rise in a warm place until doubled in size, about 1 hour.
  5. Roll the dough into a rectangle. Spread butter and sprinkle evenly with sugar/cinnamon mixture.
  6. Roll up the dough and cut into 12 rolls.
  7. Place rolls in a lightly greased pan and cover them for 30 minutes to rise.
  8. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 20 minutes or until golden brown.

Cheese Scone

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold butter, cubed
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 3/4 cup milk

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cut in the butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Stir in the grated cheese.
  5. Add milk and stir until the dough comes together.
  6. Turn out onto a floured surface and knead briefly.
  7. Pat dough into a round shape and cut into wedges.
  8. Place on a baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.

Spinach and Feta Pastry

Ingredients

  • 1 sheet puff pastry
  • 1 cup cooked spinach, drained
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix spinach, feta cheese, onions, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roll out puff pastry and cut into squares.
  4. Spoon the spinach mixture into the center of each square.
  5. Fold the squares diagonally to form triangles and press the edges to seal.
  6. Brush the tops with beaten egg.
  7. Bake for 25 minutes or until golden and puffed.
